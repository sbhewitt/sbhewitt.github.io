Analyzing Audio Features and Predicting Popularity and Genres

For my Data Science capstone, I utilized the Spotify API to collect 10 years of song data. Each song has 14 audio features that I used to predict popularity. Audio features were used to classify songs as popular and to respective genres.

Predicting Obesity Based On Lifestyle

Working in a team of three, my colleagues and I utilized health lifestlye data provided by the CDC to predict obesity. We also used survey result dataset to classify each participants into group of healthy and obese. Reddit Natural Language Processing

Thousands of posts from r/books and r/Fantasy were scraped to form two classes using the Pushshift Reddit API. After cleaning and stop-word removal, post content was analyzed using 1- and 2-word tokens from count vectorization. The resulting logistic regression model correctly identified the subreddit of 87% of the posts it had not previously seen
